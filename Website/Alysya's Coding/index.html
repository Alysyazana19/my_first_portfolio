I'm a Cat Parent 🐈

Cat lover is a person who loves cats and enjoys their company. They may have one or more cats as pets and often go out of their way to care for them and provide them with a comfortable and loving home. Cat lovers may also be involved in cat-related activities, such as volunteering at animal shelters or participating in cat shows. Overall, being a cat lover is about having a deep appreciation for these fascinating and beloved animals.

Who’s Behind the Paws 🐈‍⬛ ྀི

This page is inspired by my cat, Dori Watanabe. When he was just a tiny kitten, we couldn’t bear to see him all alone. Caring for him taught me and my family the true meaning of love and responsibility. Dori has blessed us with rezeki, joy, and countless happy moments. Because of him, our family has grown even closer, and our hearts have opened wider. Through this page, I hope to share that same love, so that every cat can feel the warmth, care, and belonging that Dori brought into our lives 🌍💖
